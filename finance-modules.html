Introduction to Stock Market

Stock market is a marketplace where you can buy and sell the stocks of a company listed on the stock exchange. You can learn about it and start investing a part of your income into the stock market to earn returns. This module is designed to help you understand the basics of the stock market so that you can start your stock investment journey soon!

Introduction to Mutual Funds

A mutual fund is a financial vehicle that takes in the money from a large number of investors and creates a pool of fund. This money is then used to purchase a number of securities like stocks of a company, government bonds, and other such instruments. Read this module to learn the basics of mutual fund investing!

Introduction to Investing

Investing is when you buy some assets with your money that have potential to benefit you in the future. Such assets include real estate, stocks, cryptocurrencies, and so on. Making an investment plan is one of the most activities to do to fulfil your financial goals. This module will help you understand the importance of investing and how you can start it!

Introduction to Insurance

Insurance protects you against losses of any kind. The most common types of personal insurance are auto, health, homeowners, and life. Having an insurance is the most important thing you can do to protect your money from mishaps. Before choosing a policy, it is better to understand how insurance works. This module will help you understand everything about insurance.

Introduction to Derivatives Market

The derivatives markets is the financial market for trading instruments that are derived from other forms of assets. Some types of derivatives include future contracts and option contracts. This module will give you a clear picture about the intricacies of the derivative markets.

Introduction to Income Tax

Income tax is the tax you pay to the government basis your income. If you are a business owner, then you have to pay an income tax on your profits. This money is then used by the government to develop the infrastructure of the country as well as pay the employees of the government owned institutions. Read this module to understand the basics of income tax.

Introduction to Commodity Trading

Commodities are goods that you can exchange for other goods. Some examples of commodities are grains, gold, beef, oil and natural gas. As an investor, you can diversify your portfolio by investing in commodities. Read this module to get an in-depth knowledge about the commodity market and how you can trade in that market.

Introduction to Cryptocurrency

Cryptocurrency is a token for an amount of cash stored in a decentralised mechanism. The underlying technology of such a currency is known as the blockchain. The cryptocurrencies are stored in different computers throughout a network. Blockchain is the technology that enables such networks of computers. This module will help you understand more about this upcoming technology.
